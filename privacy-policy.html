Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2026

Bahiyya Dhikr (“the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

Information We Collect

Location Data (When In Use)
The App requests access to your device location only while the app is in use. This is used solely to calculate accurate prayer times and determine Qibla direction. Location data is not stored, shared, or transmitted to external servers.

Notifications
If enabled, the App sends local notifications for prayer reminders and dhikr reminders. Notification preferences are stored locally on your device.

Data Storage

All data such as saved favourites, dhikr counts, and preferences are stored locally on your device. The App does not operate a remote server and does not collect personal accounts or user profiles.

Analytics & Tracking

The App does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking technologies. We do not collect or sell personal data.

Third-Party Services

The App may retrieve prayer time data from publicly available APIs. No personally identifiable information is transmitted.

Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if features change. Updates will be reflected on this page.
